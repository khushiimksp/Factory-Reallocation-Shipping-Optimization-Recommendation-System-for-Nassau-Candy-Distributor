# Factory-Reallocation-Shipping-Optimization-Recommendation-System-for-Nassau-Candy-Distributor

**Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing supply chain performance using historical logistics and sales data. The analysis was conducted using Tableau to evaluate delivery efficiency, profitability, and operational effectiveness across different regions, products, and shipping modes.
The objective is to identify patterns in delivery delays, analyze cost and profit relationships, and assess overall supply chain efficiency. The project also aims to develop an interactive dashboard to monitor key performance indicators and support data-driven decision-making.

**Objective**

To analyze supply chain performance and identify inefficiencies in delivery operations using data visualization techniques.
The objective includes evaluating delivery lead time, delay patterns, profitability, and shipping efficiency, and understanding how different factors such as region, product, and cost impact overall performance.

**Problem Statement**

Given a dataset containing supply chain and logistics information, the task is to analyze and evaluate system performance using data analysis techniques.
The analysis includes:
•	Identifying patterns in delivery delays and lead time 
•	Evaluating sales, profit, and cost relationships 
•	Measuring shipping efficiency and profitability 
•	Analyzing regional and product-level performance 
•	Developing an interactive dashboard for performance monitoring 

**Dataset Information**

The dataset contains supply chain records including:

•	**Order ID** – Unique identifier for each order 

•	**Order Date / Ship Date** – Used to calculate lead time 

•	**Region** – Delivery region (Pacific, Atlantic, Interior, Gulf) 

•	**Product Name** – Product category/details 

•	**Sales** – Revenue generated 

•	**Cost** – Cost incurred for delivery 

•	**Gross Profit** – Profit after cost deduction 

•	**Shipping Mode** – Delivery type 

•	**Lead Time** – Delivery duration (calculated) 



**Tools & Technologies Used**

•	**Tableau** – Dashboard development and data visualization 

•	**Calculated Fields (Tableau)** – KPI and metric calculations 

**Data Preparation**

The dataset was processed and prepared for analysis using Tableau.

•	Missing values were handled appropriately 

•	Lead Time was calculated using Order Date and Ship Date 

•	Delay Category was created using median-based classification 

•	Key calculated fields were developed, including: 

o	Profit Margin 

o	Shipping Efficiency 

o	Delay Rate 

•	Data was structured for effective visualization and dashboard integration 



**Dashboard Features & Visualizations**

KPI Cards (Key Metrics)
	
**Charts**


•	Lead Time by Region – Bar chart showing delivery time variation across regions 

•	Sales by Region – Comparison of revenue across different regions 

•	Profit by Region – Regional profitability analysis 

•	Cost vs Profit Analysis – Scatter plot showing relationship between cost and profit 

•	Product Performance – Sales and profit comparison by product 

•	Delay Analysis by Region – On-time vs delayed deliveries across regions 

•	Delivery Heatmap (Region vs Product) – Identifies high lead time combinations 


 **Key Insights**
 
 
•	 Approximately 42% of orders are delayed, indicating operational inefficiencies 

•	Lead time varies across regions, suggesting uneven logistics performance 

•	The business shows strong profitability (65.91% margin) 

•	Shipping efficiency (2.93) indicates effective cost utilization 

•	Certain regions contribute more significantly to sales and profit 

•	 A limited number of products drive the majority of revenue 

•	 Cost does not always directly correlate with profit, highlighting inefficiencies 


**Learning Outcomes**


•	Performed end-to-end supply chain data analysis 

•	Developed calculated fields for KPI measurement in Tableau 

•	Built an interactive and structured dashboard 

•	Conducted exploratory data analysis (EDA) using visualizations 

•	Identified operational inefficiencies and delay patterns 

•	Improved data storytelling and business insight generation skills 



**Conclusion**

The supply chain analysis highlights that while the business demonstrates strong sales performance and high profitability, there are significant inefficiencies in delivery operations, as indicated by the relatively high delay rate. Variations in lead time across regions and products further emphasize the need for improved logistics planning. The Tableau dashboard effectively supports data-driven decision-making by providing clear insights into performance metrics. By addressing operational inefficiencies and optimizing processes, the organization can enhance delivery performance, reduce delays, and achieve sustainable growth.
